# Energy Consumption Analysis: G7 vs BRICS

This project analyses energy consumption per person across G7 and BRICS countries using R and Quarto. The goal is to compare long-term trends in energy use and explore differences in per-capita energy consumption between developed and emerging economies.

## Project Overview

Energy consumption is closely linked to economic development, industrial activity, and infrastructure. This analysis compares historical energy consumption patterns between G7 and BRICS countries to better understand how energy demand varies between advanced economies and rapidly developing economies.

## Tools Used

- R
- tidyverse
- ggplot2
- patchwork
- gt
- Quarto
